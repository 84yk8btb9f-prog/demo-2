About Apex Consulting

Excellence in strategy and execution since 2009

Our Story

Founded in 2009 by Dr. Alexandra Dimitriou, Apex Consulting has grown from a boutique Athens-based consultancy to a premier advisory firm serving clients across Europe. Our journey began with a simple belief: businesses deserve partners who combine strategic thinking with hands-on execution.

Over 15 years, we've helped more than 250 organizations achieve transformational results. From startups to established enterprises, we bring deep expertise while learning the unique nuances of each client's business. This collaborative methodology has become our signature approach.
